Our concept

Come to our restaurant to discover a new concept that will put your taste buds to the test.

Simple menu

Our menu is simple but delicious for everyone. Enjoy our menu composed of carnivorous, vegetarian and vegan options.

Hot sauces

Our secret lies in our assortment of hot and savory sauces that accompany the dish. These are graded on the Scoville scale.

Spicy questions

The bonus with each menu is a series of questions to ask your friends. Beware ... our questions are as spicy as our sauces.

The origin of Hot Ones

Hot Ones is a concept created between friends. We wanted to find an unusual place where we could meet with friends and have fun in a less conventional way. What could be better than answering risky questions while eating things that make you tongue-tied?

And now?

Thanks to you, Hot Ones is a real success and we don't stop growing! Every day, we take on new challenges in order to offer you the best possible experience.
